Notes on Elements of Statistical Learning

4 February 2019 Machine Learning, Data Mining, Predictive Inference, Statistical Learning, Theory
Study notes of ‘Elements of Statistical Learning’ written by Hastie, Tibshirani and Friedman. I’m trying to understand the theory under the machine learning methods by rephrasing it with my own thoughts. Read the book from the authors’ website https://web.stanford.edu/~hastie/ElemStatLearn/. Another excellent book ‘An Introduction to Statistical Learning’ from http://www-bcf.usc.edu/~gareth/ISL/ could be a good introduction of machine learning methods. The supplemental videos at https://www.r-bloggers.com/in-depth-introduction-to-machine-learning-in-15-hours-of-expert-videos/ may also be helpful.
